About Me

I am Houston, Texas native born to two Nigerian parents with two younger sisters. Art is my call as well as an aspired life-long profession, I view the world through a creative lens and I create to inspire and motivate myself and furthermore others. I love innovation and have an imagination that channels a lot of ideas on how the world can be better. I work hard for what I believe in no matter the adversity, and I am burden by my emphatic drive to join others in solving the issues our society faces (preferably for me through art). My drive to code comes from a tenacious desire to learn new things and express creativity through the medium of web development. Coding carries a challenge that sparks within me the power to conceptualize a route toward success. Being a developer is skill I will continue to grow till I hit my peak in providing my knowledge and ability to wherever or whoever is receptive.
